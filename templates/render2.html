How can you best improve, compared to the best improvements in fitness?

The previous page was all about absolute fitness. On the next page, we look at things that athletes did to get fitter, compared to what you did to achieve your last PB. Again, this is all relative to what the model determines important. The list is ordered by a combination of the importance of this aspect in anyone's training, and your performance in this aspect. So the higher up the item, the more you need to work on it according to the model. Here, the information is even more useful than before, because these all show how athletes ran a better PB, regardless where they started off.
