This analysis uses a machine learning model, run on all athletes' Strava data, to determine the most effective factors in a running plan over 3 months leading up to their PBs.

When you upload your data, your data adds to the model, and you can see your most important factors in your training plan relative to that of top runners. From this, you can figure out where you fall short. Do you not run enough long runs? Do you need to get more distance in? Do you need to take it easier?

See my info

Warning: You need more than 3 months' data, at least one race where you scored a Strava PB, and more than 20 runs to get an analysis - otherwise, there is insufficient data to make any conclusions about your training plan.
